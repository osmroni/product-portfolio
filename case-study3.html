Case Study: New SaaS Product for Data Analysis

Impact Summary

  • Successfully delivered a new SaaS product with precise roadmap and strategy
  • Built with agile execution while maintaining strategic clarity
  • High adoption and engagement post-launch

Problem

Needed structured roadmap and strategy to deliver a new data analysis product with precision and clarity.

Discovery

Observed user behavior, gathered visits data, and analyzed workflows to continuously update reality picture for strategic decisions.

Solution

Led roadmapping and product strategy with 2 junior PMs, iterative updates based on user visits, PRDs, and close collaboration with UX, product analysts, and engineers.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Led end-to-end product strategy and roadmap
  • Mentored 2 junior PMs through iterative delivery process
  • Collaborated with UX, analysts, and engineers using agile methodology
  • Defined KPIs and tracked adoption and engagement

Results

  • Product delivered with high adoption and engagement
  • Maintained strategic clarity throughout agile execution
  • Set foundation for iterative improvements based on real user behavior
