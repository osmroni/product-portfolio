Case Study: Material Review SaaS

Impact Summary

  • Boosted active users
  • Expanded into a new workflow area for users
  • Replaced legacy system, modernized UX, and improved adoption

Problem

Outdated system limiting adoption and workflow coverage. Users were unable to leverage the full potential of the platform.

Discovery

Conducted user interviews, workflow analysis, and adoption metrics review to understand friction and identify areas for improvement.

Solution

Replaced the legacy system, streamlined workflows, and introduced a small product pivot to open a new area of expertise for users.

Role & Responsibilities

  • End-to-end product lifecycle management: discovery → roadmap → PRDs → delivery → iteration
  • Collaborated closely with UX, engineers, analysts, and stakeholders
  • Defined KPIs and tracked adoption and engagement metrics

Results

  • Boosted active users
  • Expanded workflow adoption into new areas
  • Restored usability and positioned product for future growth
