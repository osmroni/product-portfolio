Case Study: Data Product Restoration & Strategy

Impact Summary

  • Recovered lost users
  • Restored data usability across B2B2C pipelines
  • Improved adoption and trust in the data product

Problem

Core data item was unusable, causing downstream issues in pipelines with hundreds of thousands of outbound users and hundreds of inbound users.

Discovery

Analyzed pipelines, user feedback, and data flows to understand structural issues and identify step-by-step fixes required to restore usability.

Solution

Rebuilt the product strategy, implementing a step-by-step restructuring process to make the data usable again and regain lost users.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Led strategy definition and step-by-step execution for data restructuring
  • Collaborated with engineering, analysts, and stakeholders
  • Defined KPIs and tracked recovery progress

Results

  • Recovered lost users
  • Data product usable again across all pipelines
  • Increased confidence and adoption for B2B2C workflows
