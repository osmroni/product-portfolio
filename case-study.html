Case Study: Legacy Product Redesign

Problem

A legacy user-facing system suffered from low adoption, fragmented workflows, and high cognitive load, limiting engagement and scalability.

Discovery

Conducted interviews, usability testing, and funnel analysis to identify friction points across key user journeys.

Solution

Simplified core flows, unified navigation, and introduced progressive disclosure. Partnered closely with design and engineering to iterate quickly.

Impact

  • 3× increase in active users
  • +40% task completion rate
  • Foundation for future feature velocity
