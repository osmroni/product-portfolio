Case Study: Legacy Product Redesign

Impact Summary

  • 3× increase in active users
  • +40% task completion
  • Restored usability at scale and set foundation for future features

Problem

The product suffered from low adoption, fragmented workflows, and high cognitive load. This limited engagement and prevented scaling key user journeys.

Discovery

Conducted interviews, usability tests, and funnel analysis to identify friction points across critical workflows. Gathered feedback from end-users and stakeholders to prioritize improvements.

Solution

Simplified core flows, unified navigation, and introduced progressive onboarding tutorials. Partnered closely with UX and engineering teams to iterate rapidly, ensuring alignment with business and user needs.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Led end-to-end product lifecycle: discovery, roadmap, PRDs, delivery, and iteration
  • Collaborated with UX, engineering, analytics, and stakeholders
  • Defined and tracked KPIs for adoption, engagement, and operational performance

Results

  • 3× active users and 40% higher task completion
  • Restored usability at scale and improved adoption in key workflows
  • Created scalable foundation for future features and iterations
